How do I build VCs?
Standards, platforms and architecture — tailored to your sector.

Recommended Standards

W3C VC Data ModelISO 18013-5 mDLOID4VC

Open-Source DPG Platforms

INJI
MOSIP-aligned wallet and issuance platform. Proven at national scale in multiple countries.
walt.id
Enterprise-grade SSI stack. Full W3C VC + OID4VC support with REST APIs.
Why these choices: National ID requires both physical (mDL) and digital (W3C VC) formats. OID4VC ensures wallet interoperability. INJI provides a proven national-scale deployment path; walt.id suits custom enterprise integration.

The 4-Layer Architecture Stack

L1
Identifiers
DID:web or DID:jwk for government issuers. Linked to existing National ID registry.
L2
Format
W3C VC JSON-LD for digital presentation + ISO mDL for physical/mobile driver use.
L3
Transport
OID4VCI for issuance, OID4VP for presentation. Works across wallets.
L4
Trust
Government-run Trust Registry. Anchored to national PKI infrastructure.
